About Me

Hi, I'm Charlie Glennon, a passionate videographer with a strong focus on sports content. While I'm also a dedicated college baseball player, previously at Denison University and now at Division 1 University of Maryland, I am driven by the art of visual storytelling and thrive in creative environments. I love capturing moments that bring the excitement of sports to life, from live editing to creating engaging social media content.

With a background in both multimedia journalism, economics, and high level sports, I bring a unique blend of creativity and analytical thinking to my work. My experiences at Warner Bros./Discovery, Denison University Athletics, the Cincinnati Reds, and the Univrsity of Maryland have allowed me to fine-tune my skills and learn so much along the way.

My work has generated over 5 million impressions across social media platforms and I look to continue to grow the reach of any team, brand, company, or organziation that I am a part of.

I have served as a Senior Content Specialist/Videographer at Denison University Athletics as well as currently as a Creative Video Production intern for Maryland Athletics. I have taken on both of these roles as a full time student and NCAA Athlete. My time management and ability to work on deadline are some of my best skills.

My internship with the Cincinnati Reds allowed me to hone my skills in real-time video content creation, from hype videos to player highlights and interview specials. With exerience ranging from a small scale Division 3 school to a major production company and a professional sports team , my experience is well-rounded and very diverse. I'm well-versed in using Adobe Creative Suite, including After Effects, Premiere Pro, Photoshop, and Audition. I have a knack for producing high-quality video content and excelling in audience engagement and analytics.

I'm currently pursuing a Certificate in Multimedia Journalism at the University of Maryland and hold a Bachelor of Arts degree in Economics from Denison University. These academic experiences have equipped me with a holistic understanding of media world and have put me in a postition to use my skills to help teams, companies, businesses, brands, and individuals reach vast audiences.
